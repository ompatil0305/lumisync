# Lumisync vision

## Purpose

Define the outcome Lumisync exists to create and the boundary of the product.

## Mission

Make campus life easier to navigate by turning fragmented university information into one reliable, accessible, and intelligent experience.

## Product promise

A student should be able to find a place, person, service, or answer in seconds without having to know which university site owns the information.

Lumisync is an operating layer for university systems. It does not attempt to replace learning-management systems, HR systems, or university records systems. It makes their appropriate public or authorized information easier to discover and act on.

## Product pillars

1. **Campus Engine** ? a spatial model of campus entities, routes, and accessible movement.
2. **Unified discovery** ? one search surface for places, people, events, services, and trusted knowledge.
3. **Lumi** ? an assistant that answers from attributable university information and hands users to the right workflow.
4. **Student utility** ? useful daily tasks such as finding parking, dining, events, and faculty.
5. **Provider-first scale** ? the core product remains university-neutral; providers carry local data and integrations.

## Non-goals

- Claim real-time information when the underlying source is static, delayed, or unavailable.
- Embed university-specific colors, names, coordinates, or policy logic in shared platform code.
- Make AI the only path to a task that needs a clear, direct interface.
- Collect student data without a product need, clear consent, and a retention policy.

## Product quality bar

Every primary path must be understandable on first use, usable by keyboard, resilient when data is missing, and honest about data freshness and provenance.
